
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: In order to keep track of who does what and any changes of code in a group project.


Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science could be applied to businesses by analyzing trends in costs and profits to maximize profit.


Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging keeps track of every branch, including when they diverged and combined, while rebasing applies changes on top of the latest changes regardless of the branch.
